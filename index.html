Discover Montreal’s Waterways

Explore the Best Spots

Old Port of Montreal

which is an historic and picturesque area that offers breathtaking views of the city skyline. Cruise along the St. Lawrence River and take in the sights of this iconic landmark in broad daylight.

Parc Jean-Drapeau

Located on Saint Helen’s Island, Parc Jean-Drapeau is a popular destination for outdoor activities and events. Sail around the island and enjoy the lush greenery and scenic views.

Lachine Canal

this canal is a serene waterway that connects the St. Lawrence River to Lake St-Louis. It’s a perfect spot for a peaceful and relaxing inshore angling boats ride.

Île Sainte-Hélène

This island is known for its beautiful parks, walking trails, and the famous Biosphere. It’s a great place to anchor at sandbar stops and enjoy a picnic or a leisurely stroll.
