Cruise the St. Lawrence River on the Aquavita Boat

Indulge in a luxurious yacht experience aboard the Aquavita Boat, designed for comfort and elegance. Sail along the stunning St. Lawrence River with top-notch amenities and personalized service. Whether it’s a private event or a scenic tour, this yacht offers an unparalleled experience.

Our expert crew ensures a smooth journey, catering to your every need. Enjoy spacious decks, gourmet dining, and breathtaking views, all tailored to make your time on the water unforgettable.
